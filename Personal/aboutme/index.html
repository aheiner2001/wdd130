About Me

Aaron Heiner

My name is Aaron Heiner. I was born and raised in Nampa, Idaho There are nine people in my family with me being the 3rd child.

I played tennis in middle school and have enjoyed playing that but about 3 years ago I discovered pickleball which our family has loved to play. My current major is software development and I hope that I enjoy it enough to stick with it.

I, with my family, have been a member of the church my whole life. I served a mission in the north of Chile. I grew to love the people and the food down there which consists of hotdogs with lots of mayo.
